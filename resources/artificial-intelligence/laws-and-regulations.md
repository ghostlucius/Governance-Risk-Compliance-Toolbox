# Artificial Intelligence Laws and Regulations

## EU AI Regulation

- [Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 - Artificial Intelligence Act](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/HTML/?uri=OJ:L_202401689)
  Brief description: The EU AI Act establishes a risk-based regulatory framework for AI systems, including prohibited practices, obligations for high-risk systems, transparency duties, and governance requirements.
  Topic: AI compliance, risk classification, governance audits
