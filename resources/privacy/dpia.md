# DPIA and PIA

## Guidance

- [OAIC eLearning: Undertaking a Privacy Impact Assessment](https://education.oaic.gov.au/elearning/pia/welcome.html)
  Brief description: An introductory OAIC course that walks users through the privacy impact assessment process, including the main steps, risk identification, and mitigation planning.
  Topic: PIA, training, practical guidance

- [EDPB DPIA Template](https://www.edpb.europa.eu/our-work-tools/documents/public-consultations/2026/edpb-dpia-template_en)
  Brief description: The EDPB's 2026 draft DPIA template released for public consultation, intended to support a common template or meta-template across supervisory authorities.
  Topic: DPIA, accountability, templates

## Worksheets And Templates

- [OAIC PIA Worksheet (available from the OAIC eLearning course)](https://education.oaic.gov.au/elearning/pia/welcome.html)
  Brief description: A downloadable worksheet that helps users begin documenting a privacy impact assessment while working through the OAIC training.
  Topic: PIA, worksheet, assessment support
