# Cybersecurity and Resilience

## Security Guidance

- [CNIL Practice Guide for the Security of Personal Data: 2024 edition](https://www.cnil.fr/en/practice-guide-security-personal-data-2024-edition)
  Brief description: A practical CNIL guide on security measures for personal data processing, updated in 2024 with new factsheets on artificial intelligence, cloud computing, mobile applications, APIs, and data management security.
  Topic: Cybersecurity, GDPR Article 32, compliance audits

- [NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5, Security and Privacy Controls for Information Systems and Organizations](https://csrc.nist.gov/pubs/sp/800/53/r5/upd1/final)
  Brief description: NIST's control catalog for security and privacy programs, widely used to structure risk management, internal controls, assessments, and audit-ready governance.
  Topic: Security controls, privacy controls, audits, governance

## EU Cybersecurity Regulation

- [Regulation (EU) 2022/2554 - Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA)](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/HTML/?uri=CELEX:32022R2554)
  Brief description: The EU regulation on digital operational resilience for the financial sector, covering ICT risk management, incident reporting, resilience testing, and third-party risk oversight.
  Topic: Financial sector cybersecurity, operational resilience, regulatory compliance

- [Directive (EU) 2022/2555 - NIS 2 Directive](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/HTML/?uri=CELEX:32022L2555)
  Brief description: The EU directive establishing a higher common level of cybersecurity across the Union, including governance, incident reporting, supervision, and accountability requirements for essential and important entities.
  Topic: Cybersecurity governance, incident reporting, compliance audits

## Related Privacy Resources

- [EDPB Guidelines 9/2022 on personal data breach notification under GDPR](https://www.edpb.europa.eu/our-work-tools/our-documents/guidelines/guidelines-92022-personal-data-breach-notification-under_en)
  Brief description: Guidance on how to assess, document, and notify personal data breaches under the GDPR, with practical value for incident response and audit preparation.
  Topic: Data breaches, incident response, GDPR

- [CNIL Recommendation on the use of APIs for the secure sharing of personal data (French)](https://www.cnil.fr/sites/cnil/files/2023-07/recommandation_api.pdf)
  Brief description: CNIL technical recommendations for using APIs to share personal data securely, with a focus on security controls and data protection by design.
  Topic: APIs, secure data sharing, technical compliance
