# Templates and Worksheets

## Available Resources

- [OAIC PIA Worksheet (available from the OAIC eLearning course)](https://education.oaic.gov.au/elearning/pia/welcome.html)
  Brief description: A downloadable worksheet that helps users begin documenting a privacy impact assessment while working through the OAIC training.
  Topic: PIA, worksheet, assessment support

- [EDPB DPIA Template](https://www.edpb.europa.eu/our-work-tools/documents/public-consultations/2026/edpb-dpia-template_en)
  Brief description: A draft EDPB template intended to support structured, repeatable data protection impact assessments across organizations and authorities.
  Topic: DPIA, template, assessment support
